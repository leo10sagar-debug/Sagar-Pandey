Summary

I am currently pursuing an MBA in Finance at Uniglobe College, Kathmandu (affiliated with Pokhara University), and hold a Bachelor's degree in Statistics. My academic background has developed my analytical thinking and strengthened my interest in using data to understand financial performance and support better decision-making.

I'm passionate about financial analysis, corporate finance, and investment research — analyzing financial statements, evaluating business performance, and exploring how quantitative methods can solve real-world financial problems. My foundation in statistics lets me approach finance from a data-driven perspective.

My long-term goal is to pursue a PhD in Finance and contribute to research that advances financial analysis and evidence-based decision-making. I'm always eager to connect with researchers, academics, and finance professionals to exchange ideas and explore collaboration.
